About Us

Local Recipes is a platform dedicated to preserving and sharing the traditional dishes of Uganda.
We celebrate the flavors that bring families together and showcase recipes passed down through generations.

The Problem Statement

Many Local Recipes are being forgotten because they are not well documented or shared widely.

"As modern foods become more popular, younger generations are losing touch with their cultural dishes.
The website aims to preserve these recipes and make them easily accessible for everyone."

Our Mission

To promote and preserve Local Recipes by creating a simple, educational and accessible platform.
To help people learn how to prepare and enjoy authentic dishes from our regions.

Our Vision

To become the leading online platform that connects communities through Local Recipes.
Celebrating our cultural heritage and inspiring future generations to value Local Recipes.

Our Objectives

  • To collect and document Local Recipes from various regions.
  • To educate people on how to prepare heathly and authentic Local Recipes.
  • To inspire younger generations to appreciate their Local foods.
  • To provide easy online access to Local Recipes for home cooks and other learners.
  • To promote cultural identity through sharing.
