Sombe

Prep Time:15mins
Cook Time:30-40mins
Servings:4 people

The Story Behind the Dish

A light, mild spice mix seasoning blend depending on its usage.

Ingredients

  • Cassava leaves(fresh or frozen)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup of groundnuts (optional)
  • Palm oil or vegetable oil
  • Salt for taste

Instructions

  1. Step1:Saute onion,tomatoes in oil;
  2. Step2:Add cassavaleaves (and water as needed)
  3. Step3:Simmer until leaves are tender; stir in groundnuts for richness.
  4. Step4:Season for taste.
