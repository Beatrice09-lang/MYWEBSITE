Irish potatoes

Prep Time:10mins
Cook Time:20-25mins
Servings:2-3 people

The Story Behind the Dish

This is a flavorful dish with basic ingredients and easy to prepare.
Combining tender potatoes with onions,garlic,and tomatoes in a savory mix

Ingredients

  • 2-3 irish potatoes(peeled and diced)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 onion(chopped)
  • 1 tomato(diced)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optionasl:spices like paprika,chili powder for added flavor

Instructions

  1. Step1:Heat oil in a pan, saute onions and garlic until soft.
  2. Step2:Add diced potatoes and cook until slightly browned.
  3. Step3:Add diced tomato and stir.
  4. Step4:Season with salt,pepper,and optional spices.
  5. Step5:Cook until potatoes are tender.
