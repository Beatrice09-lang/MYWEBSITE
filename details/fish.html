Smoked Fish

Prep Time:10mins
Cook Time:Varies(smoking time)
Servings:Serve as staples, in sauces, or as a snack

The Story Behind the Dish

Fish preserved and flavored through smoking.

Ingredients

  • 1kg of fish(like tilapia or Nile perch)
  • 2 tablespoons of salt
  • 1 tea spoon of spices(like black pepper)

Instructions

  1. Step1:Clean and gut the fish
  2. Step2:Brine the fish in salt water or a mixture of salt,sugar and spices.
  3. Step3:Smoke the fish over low heat using wood chips or chunks until cooked through
  4. Step4:Serve hot or store for later use.
