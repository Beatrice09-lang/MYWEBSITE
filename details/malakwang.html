Malakwang

Prep Time:10mins
Cook Time:40mins
Servings:6 people

The Story Behind the Dish

This is absolutely delicious Acholi,Luo,Ugandan dish.Sour and sweet usually go well together that's why Malakwang and sweet potatoes are a perfect match and taste good.

Ingredients

    Malakwang with peanut butter sauce

  • 300 grams of Dry Malakwang
  • 340 grams of smooth peanut butter
  • 1/2 tin tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1,5 litres of water Add as needed
  • Boiled sweet potatoes
  • 1kg of Freah Ugandan sweet potatoes
  • 0.5 litre water

Instructions

  1. Step1:Boil water in a pan.
  2. Step2:Add the dry malakwang leaves and cook for 15 minutes.
  3. Step3:Test for softeness by pressing a bit of leaves against a wooden spoon.
  4. Step4:Drain the first water completely and then, add fresh water plus tomatoes.
  5. Step5:Boil for 5minutes.Then add salt and mix well for taste.
  6. Step6:Add peanut butter and serve while its still hotwith sweet potatoes.
  7. Step7:Can also be eaten with millet bread, maize meal, and boiled cassava.

  8. Step8:Peel sweet potatoes, wash throughly and boil them.
  9. Step9:Cut them in desired sizes.
  10. Step10:Boil in a large saucepan enough to contain them well for 20-25minutes.
  11. Step11:Poke with a knife to test for softness.
  12. Step12:Serve with Malakwang.
