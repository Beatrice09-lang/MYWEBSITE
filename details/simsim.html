Simsim

Prep Time:About 10mins roasting and 3mins caramelizing
Cook Time:About 10mins roasting and 3mins caramelizing
Servings:4-6 people

The Story Behind the Dish

Roasted sesame seeds caramelized with sugar to make crunchy sesame snaps, a popuplar Ugandan snack.

Ingredients

  • 500g of roasted sesame seeds
  • 4 tbsp of sugar
  • A half tsp of salt for taste

Instructions

  1. Step1:Grease a tray for setting the simsim.
  2. Step2:Roast the sesame seeds with salt for about 10mins, stirring continuously to avoid burning.
  3. Step3:In another pan, add sugar and let it caramelize for about 3minutes, stirring constantly until golden.
  4. Step4:Quickly mix the roasted sesame seeds into the caramelized sugar until the mixture clumps together.
  5. Step5:Scoop the mixture onto the greased tray, spread evenly, and press down quickly.
  6. Step6:Cut into desired shapes and allow to cool for 5minutes before serving.
