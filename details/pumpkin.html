Pumpkin

Prep Time:10mins
Cook Time:20-30mins
Servings:According to size cooked

The Story Behind the Dish

Commonly steamed or cooked with banana leaves wrappings; pumpkin seeds can also be roasted and eaten for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Pumpkin pieces
  • optional(banana leaves for steaming)
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Step1: Place the pumpkin sliced pieces in a steamer or pot with little water.
  2. Step2: Cover and steam until fork-tender.
