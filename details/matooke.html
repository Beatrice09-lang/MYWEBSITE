Stewed Matooke

Prep Time:15mins
Cook Time:30mins
Servings:4 people

The Story Behind the Dish

Stewed Matooke is a traditional dish made from green bananas cooked in rich tomato and onion sauce.
Its hearty, flavorful, and often served with beef or beans.

Ingredients

  • 10 Matooke peeled
  • 1/2 cups of water
  • 1 medium chopped onion
  • cooking oil
  • 2 tomatoes chopped
  • 1 tea spoon of grated garlic
  • 1 tea spoon of grated ginger
  • 1 small green chili (optional)
  • 1 stock cube(e.g,Royco)
  • Salt for taste
  • 1/2 tea spoon(s) of turmeric(optional)
  • Fresh coriander(for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Step1 :Prepare Matooke: Peel the matooke and place them in salted water to prevent browning.
  2. Step2:Make the base: Heat oil in a pan. Add onions and saute until golden.
  3. Step3:Add Aromatics: Stir in garlic, ginger, and chili. Cook for 1minute.
  4. Step4:Tomatoes and Spices: Add tomatoes andturmeric (if using). Cook until tomatoes breakdown.
