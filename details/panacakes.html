Fluffy pancakes(Kabalagala)

Prep Time:10-15 mins
Cook Time:10-15mins
Servings: 4(roughly) 2-4 depending on the size and asppetite.

The Story Behind the Dish

Ugandan banana-cassava pancake-thin sweet often cooked in a flat-round style ,commonly served as a snack.

Ingredients

  • Ripe bananas,cassava flour
  • A pinch of salt,sugar(optional),baking powder
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Step1:Mash ripe bananas until smooth.
  2. Step2:Mix in cassava flour gradually to form a thick batter; add a little water if to dry.
  3. Step3:Season with salt and a touch of sugar if desired.
  4. Step4:Heat a lightly oiled flat pan over medium heat.
  5. Step5:Pour batter to form small pancakes; cook until bubbles form and edges set, flip and cook until golden.
