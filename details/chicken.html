Chicken sauce

Prep Time:Slow cooking of chicken pieces in seasoned tomato sauce
Cook Time:40-60mins depending on tenderness required
Servings:4-6 depending on family meals

The Story Behind the Dish

Chicken sauce cooked with many ingredients and spices served with dishes(like rice,matooke,posho).

Ingredients

  • chicken pieces
  • onions and tomatoes,chopped
  • garlic,minced
  • cooking oil
  • salt and pepper
  • water or stock
  • Curry powder, ground coriander or other spices

Instructions

  1. Step1:Brown the chicken in oil; remove and set aside.
  2. Step2:In the same pot, saute onions and garlic until fragrant.
  3. Step3:Add tomatoes and cook until the sauce thickens.
  4. Step4:Return chicken pieces, add water to just cover, season, and simmer until chicken is tender and sauce thickens(about 30-45mins).
  5. Step5:Adjust seasoning and serve with matooke ,rice,posho.
