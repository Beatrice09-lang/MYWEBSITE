Roasted maize

Prep Time:5mins
Cook Time:10-15mins
Servings:Varies (typically served as a snack)

The Story Behind the Dish

Street food made from fresh corn roasted over an open flame.
Giving it a smoky, chewy and slightly charred flavor.

Ingredients

  • Fresh corn
  • Salt for taste

Instructions

  1. Step1:Grill or roast fresh corn over an open flame until slightly charred.
  2. Step2:Sprinkle with salt for taste.
