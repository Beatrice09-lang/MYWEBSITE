Nakati

Prep Time:5mins
Cook Time:5-7 mins
Servings:4 people

The Story Behind the Dish

A leafy green vegetable cooked with onions and tomatoes.

Ingredients

  • Nakati greens, chopped
  • 1 onion and tomato, chopped
  • Oil and Salt(for taste)

Instructions

  2. Step2:Add greens; cook until wilted.
