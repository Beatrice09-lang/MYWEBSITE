Chapati(flat bread)

Prep Time:15mins
Cook Time:15mins
Servings:6 people

The Story Behind the Dish

Flat,round unleaved bread cooked on a hot griddle; widely enjoyed acrossdifferent areas.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of all- purpose flour
  • 3/4 cups of warm water
  • 1/2 tbsp of oil

Instructions

  1. Step1:Mix flour,salt and water to form a soft dough;
  2. Step2:Knead for 15-20minutes and rest for 15-20 minutes
  3. Step3:Roll into thin circles; cook on a hot skillet 1-2minutes per side.
