Dodo

Prep Time:15mins
Cook Time:30-40mins
Servings:4 people

The Story Behind the Dish

Greens commonly served as a side dish ,sauteed with onions,garlic.

Ingredients

  • Dodo leaves,chopped
  • 2 onions,chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic,minced
  • A half cup of water or stock
  • Salt for taste

Instructions

  1. Step1:Saute onion and garlic;
  2. Step2:Add leaves and water,salt; simmer until tender and stir to mix them
  3. Step3:Season
