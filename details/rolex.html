Rolex

Prep Time:10-15mins to prep ingredients
Cook Time:20-30mins
Servings:1-2 people

The Story Behind the Dish

A popular Ugandan street food consisting of a rolled chapati ,stuffed with an egg omelet and vegetables.
Its cheap,fast and out-of-this-world tasty.

Ingredients

  • For chapatis: flour,salt for taste,water,oil
  • For fillings:eggs,onions,tomatoes,cabbages,carrots

Instructions

  1. Step1:Prepare the chapati dough by mixing flour,salt,waterand oil.
  2. Step2:Let it rest,then roll and fry until golden.
  3. Step3:Beat eggs and mix with chopped veggies.
  4. Step4:Cook the omelet then roll it up in the chapati.
