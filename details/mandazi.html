Mandazi

Prep Time:20mins
Cook Time:12-15mins
Servings:12 small pieces

The Story Behind the Dish

Lightly sweet; fluffy doughnuts with slightly crispy exterior and soft,airy interior.
Typically triangular in shape though sometimes round or oval accordingly.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2tbsp sugar
  • 1tsp dry yeast
  • 1/2 cups of coconut milk or warm water
  • 1/4 cups of melted butter or oil
  • Pinch of salt
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Step1:Activate yeast in warm milk /water with a pinch of sugar for 5-10minutes.
  2. Step2:Mix dry ingredients; knead until smooth.
  3. Step3:Let rise 30-45minutes.
  4. Step4:Roll to 1/4 inch thick, cut into diamonds or triangles; fry until golden.
