Sausages

Prep Time:15-20mins
Cook Time:30-40mins
Servings:4-6people

The Story Behind the Dish

Seasoned meat mixtures stuffed into casings or shaped.
Can be grilled, pan-fried or smoked offering the savory and often spicy flavor.

Ingredients

  • 500g minced meat(beef,pork or a combination of both)
  • 1 large onion,finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic,minced
  • 1/2 tsps ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp ground ginger and coriander
  • Salt 1 teaspoon for taste
  • Natural sausage casings(optional)

Instructions

  1. Step1:Mix minced meat,chopped onions,minced garlic,ginger,coriander,salt and black pepper in a bowl until well combined.
  2. Step2:Stuff the mixture into natural sausage casings, if using or shape into patties.
  3. Step3:Cook the sausages in boiling water for 30 minutes or grill until browned.
  4. Step4:Serve hot with matooke,ugali or even side of vegetables.
