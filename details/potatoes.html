Sweet potatoes

Prep Time:10mins peeling and cutting
Cook Time:25mins boiling
Servings:4-6 people

The Story Behind the Dish

Boiled or roasted sweet potatoes commonly eaten as staple food.

Ingredients

  • Fresh sweet potatoes
  • water
  • salt
  • Optional; oil for frying if frying slices

Instructions(Boiled)

  1. Step1:Scrub and peel (optional) sweet potatoes.
  2. Step2:Boil in salted water until fork-tender,about 15-25minutes.
  3. Step3:Drain and serve while hot.

Instructions(fried)

  1. **Step1:Slice into thick coins or wedges; heat oil in a pan.
    **Step2:Fry until golden and tender; drain and season with salt.
