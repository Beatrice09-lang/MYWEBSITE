Beef sauce

Prep Time:15mins
Cook Time:30mins
Servings:4 people

The Story Behind the Dish

Commonly prepared as stews or braises.
Beef is often simmered with onions. tomatoes,peppers and spices.

Ingredients

  • 500g of ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 gloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 400g crushed tomastoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1teaspoon of paprika
  • Saltb and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • Freshly parsley, chopped (optional)

Instructions

  1. Step1:Heat oil in a pan;saute' onion and garlic until translucent.
  2. Step2:Add beef; cook until browned.
  3. Step3:Stir in pepper, paprika, cumin; cook 2minutes.
  4. Step4:Add tomato paste and crushed tomatoes; simmer 20-25 minutes.
  5. Step5:Season with salt, pepper; finish with parsley.
