Kikomando

Prep Time:5-10mins
Cook Time:15-20mins
Servings:2-3

The Story Behind the Dish

Street-food style combo where items are assebled together, sometimes with fried plantains or bread.

Ingredients

  • Millet flour
  • Water or broth,oil
  • Salt for taste
  • Optional side items(salad,sauce,or fried items)

Instructions (Basic)

  1. Step1:Prepare a starchy base by cooking flour with water to a thick porridge consistency.
  2. Step2:Season lightly; serve with your chosen sides or toppings.
