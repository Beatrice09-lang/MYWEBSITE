Malewa

Prep Time:20mins
Cook Time:45mins
Servings:4 people

The Story Behind the Dish

Dried bamboo shoots cooked with groundnut paste and salt.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of dried bamboo shoots
  • 1 cup of groundnut paste(peanut butter can substitute)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon of salt(for taste)
  • 2 cups of warm water
  • 1 tablespoon of cooking oil (optional)
  • A half teaspoon of bicarbonate of soda(optional, for softening)

Instructions

  1. Step1:Break the dried bamboo shoots into pieces and soak them in warm water for about 15-20 minutes to soften.
  2. Step2:Drain the soaking water, add fresh water, boil the shoots for 20-30 minutes until tender.
    (You can add bicarbonate of soda for quick softening.)
  3. Step3:In a separate pan, fry onions and tomatoes(optional)until soft.
    Mix the groundnut paste with warm water to make a smooth sauce.
  4. Step4:Add the boiled Malewa to the sauce and stir gently.
    5. Simmer for another 10-15minutes, adding water if necessary, until the sauce thickens and flavor blend.
  5. Step5:Best served hot with Atapa(millet bread), Matooke,Boiled cassava.
