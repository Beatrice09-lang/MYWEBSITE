Stewed cassava

Prep Time:15mins
Cook Time:25mins
Servings:4

The Story Behind the Dish

Soft-boiled cassava served with seasoned tomato stew.

Ingredients

  • Cassava
  • 2 medium sized onions, tomatoes
  • Cooking oil
  • Pepper
  • Salt for taste

Instructions

  1. Step1:Peel, chop, and boil cassava.
  2. Step2:Fry onions and tomatoes.
  3. Step3:Add cassava, season, and stew together.
