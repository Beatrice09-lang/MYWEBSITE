Kalo

Prep Time:15-25mins
Cook Time:20-40mins
Servings:4-6(depending on the size/shape)

The Story Behind the Dish

Kalo is a nutrient-dense and versatile, often used as a bread substitute or accompaniiment to soups and stews; texture ranges from soft and fluffy to firm, depending on tghe exact flour blend acd cooking method.

Ingredients

  • Millet flour(finger millet or similar)
  • Cassava flour(opyional for texture)
  • Water and salt(for taste)
  • Optional binding agents like (A little yeast or baking powder depending on the method.)

Instructions

  1. Method1:(Steamed/ kibo-style):Mix millet flour with water to form a thick batter,season with salt,steam in a lined basket or cloth until set and firm . Cool slightly,slice and serve.

  2. Method:(Baked/ roasted):Combine millet flour with water to form a dough, shape into rounds or loaf,steam or bake until firm and fully cooked. Slice and serve.
