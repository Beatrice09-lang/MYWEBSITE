Posho

Prep Time:5mins
Cook Time:15-20mins
Servings:4-6 depends on quantity made, typical for a family

The Story Behind the Dish

A thick maize meal porridge, staple Ugandan dish.

Ingredients

  • maize flour(finely ground)
  • water
  • salt (optional)

Instructions

  1. Step1:Boil water in a pot; gradually sprinkle in maize flour while stirring vigorously to avoid lumps.
  2. Step2:Reduce heat and continue stirring until mixture thickens and pulls away from the sides.
  3. Step3:Cover briefly to firm up, then serve hot as a forkable staple.
