Pilau rice

Prep Time:15mins Cook Time:30mins Servings:Serve when hot as main dish or side dish

The Story Behind the Dish

Aromatic rice dish cooked with spices, often served at special occassions.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of rice
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch of ginger, minced
  • Pilau masala, water
  • 1-2 cups of meat/vegetable broth (optional)
  • Salt for taste

Instructions

  1. Step1:Saute onions, garlic and spices in oil.
  2. Step2:Add rice and stir to coat with spices.
  3. Step3:Add liquid(water or broth) and bring to a boil.
  4. Step4:Reduce heat, cover and simmer until the rice is cooked.
  5. Step5:Fluff and serve
