Groundnut sauce

Prep Time:15mins
Cook Time:20mins
Servings: Serve hot with main dishes

The Story Behind the Dish

Rich and creamy sauce made with groundnuts(ebinyobwa), often served wuth staples like matooke,rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of groundnuts, roasted and ground into paste
  • 2 medium onions, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup(s) of water
  • 1-inch of ginger, minced
  • Salt for taste
  • 1-2 tea spoons of chili peppers(optional for heat)
  • Cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Step1:Roast groundnuts, blend into smooth paste.
  2. Step2:Saute onions, garlic, ginger in cooking oil till soft.
  3. Step3:Add tomatoes, cook till mushy.
  4. Step4:Add groundnut paste, water,salt, chili(if using).
  5. Step5:Simmer till sauce thickens.
