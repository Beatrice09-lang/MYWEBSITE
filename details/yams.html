Yams

Prep Time:10minutes
Cook Time:20-25minutes
Servings:4 people

The Story Behind the Dish

Starchy tubers commonly boiled,steamed in Ugandan cuisine; often served as a staple with sauces.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large yams,peeled and cubed
  • 1-2 tbsp of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Step1:Toss with oil, salt,pepper.
  2. Step2:Cook at 220'C(425'F) or boil until fork-tender.
  3. Step3:Serve while still hot.
