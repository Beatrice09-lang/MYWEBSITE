Silver-fish

Prep Time:10mins
Cook Time:8-12mins
Servings:4 people

The Story Behind the Dish

Small, silvery freshwater fish sold in markets; typically fried,dried or simmered in stews.

Ingredients

  • fillets of silver fish
  • 1 onion and 2 tomatoes
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Step1:Saute onion and tomatoes;garlic,simmer for 10-15minutes.
  2. Step2:Add fillets of silver fish and water as needed to reach a desired consistency.
  3. Step3:Season
