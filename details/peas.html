Cowpeas

Prep Time:10-15mins
Cook Time:20-30mins
Servings:4-6 people

The Story Behind the Dish

Often green-peas and served with posho in most cases.

Ingredients

  • peas
  • onions and tomatoes,chopped
  • cooking oil
  • garlic, minced
  • salt for taste
  • waetr or stock
  • Curry powder or other spices

Instructions

  1. Step1:Heat the oil in a pot, saute onions until translucent.
  2. Step2:Add garlic and tomatoes, cook until tomatoes break down.
  3. Step3:Stir peas and splash water; season with salt and spices.
  4. Step4:Simmer until the peas are tender and sauce thickens(about 15-25minutes).
  5. Step5:Serve hot over freshly cooked posho(maize meal porridge/porridge-like stiff dough).
