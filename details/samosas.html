Samosas

Prep Time:30minutes
Cook Time:15-20minutes
Servings:12 pieces

The Story Behind the Dish

Triangular pastry turnovers filled with spiced meat, vegetables or legumes, deep-fried until crispy; a popular snack.

Ingredients

  • For the dough;(2 cups flour,3/4 cups of water,2tbsp oil,pinch salt)
  • Filling;(200g spiced peas or minced meat)

Instructions

  1. Step1:Prepare dough; rest for 15minutes.
  2. Step2:Prepare filling(boil peas and mix with spices).
  3. Step3:Cut dough into triangles; fill and seal.
  4. Step4:Deep-fry until golden or bake at 200'C(400'F) for 15-20 minutes.
