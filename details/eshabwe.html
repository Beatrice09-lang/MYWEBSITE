Eshabwe

Prep Time:10mins
Cook Time:5mins
Servings:4-6

The Story Behind the Dish

A creamy ghee-based sauce from Ankole region, served cold with matooke or millet.

Ingredients

  • Unprocessed ghee
  • Rock salt
  • Warm water

Instructions

  1. Step1:Whisk ghee in a bowl.
  2. Step2:Slowly add warm water and salt.
  3. Step3:Continue whisking until creamy.
