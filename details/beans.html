Bean sauce

Prep Time:10mins
Cook Time:25mins
Servings:4 people

The Story Behind the Dish

Legume dish prepared in many households; often red-kidney-beans orblack-eyed peas cooked into thick stew.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried beans( or 2 cups of cooked beans)
  • 1 onion and tomato chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp of cumin and paprika
  • Salt for taste
  • 1 tbsp of oil

Instructions

  1. Step1:Saute onion and garlic; add beans,tomatoes,spices.
  2. Step2:Simmer 15-20minutes
  3. Step3:Add water if needed to reach desired consistency.
